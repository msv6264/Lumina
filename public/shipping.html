Shipping Policy

At Lumina, we provide digital educational resources, online courses, and learning materials. Below is our policy regarding the delivery of digital and physical educational materials.

Digital Products Delivery

All digital products, including e-books, online courses, and downloadable study materials, are delivered instantly upon successful payment. Customers will receive an email with access credentials or download links.

Physical Products Shipping

For physical educational materials such as printed books, study kits, and merchandise, shipping times and costs are as follows:

  • Standard Shipping: 5-7 business days
  • Express Shipping: 2-3 business days
  • International Shipping: 7-14 business days (availability varies by country)

Shipping Costs

Order Processing

Orders for digital products are processed immediately, while physical product orders are processed within 1-2 business days. Customers will receive a confirmation email with tracking details for physical shipments.

Address Accuracy

Ensure that your shipping address is correct. We are not responsible for delays due to incorrect address details provided during checkout.

Delayed or Lost Shipments

If your shipment is delayed or lost, please contact our support team. We will work with the shipping carrier to resolve the issue. Digital product access issues should also be reported to our support team.

Refunds & Replacements

Due to the nature of digital products, we do not offer refunds once access has been granted. For physical products, damaged or incorrect items can be replaced within 7 days of delivery.
